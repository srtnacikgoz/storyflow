# Coffee Prompts - Gemini Pro

> **Model:** Gemini Pro (imagen-4.0-generate-001)
> **Marka:** Sade Patisserie
> **Platform:** Instagram (Stories 9:16, Feed 4:5, Grid 1:1)

---

## Temel Kurallar

1. **Image-to-image zorunlu** - Her prompt referans görsel ile çalışır
2. **Ürün tarifi yasak** - AI referanstan öğrenir
3. **İnsan eli yok** - Varsayılan olarak yasak (istisna: POV shot)
4. **STEAM/SMOKE KESİNLİKLE YASAK** - En önemli kural!
5. **Off-white backdrop** - RGB(250,248,245) varsayılan
6. **Latte art koruması** - Referanstaki desen korunmalı

---

## Kompozisyon Varyasyonları

### A. Latte Art Hero (Overhead)

```
Using uploaded image as reference.

Professional overhead photography of latte art from reference,
strict top-down view to showcase foam design.

90-degree angle, soft even lighting,
latte art pattern sharp and detailed,
cup rim clean, saucer minimal.

1:1 square aspect ratio.

CRITICAL: Preserve exact latte art pattern from reference.

--no steam, smoke, tilted view, shadow on foam, blurry pattern
```

### B. Espresso Shot (Side View)

```
Using uploaded image as reference.

Professional food photography of espresso shot from reference,
side angle showing crema layer and glass/cup.

30-45 degree angle, soft side lighting,
rich crema color visible, glass transparency (if applicable),
clean minimal backdrop.

4:5 aspect ratio.

--no steam, smoke, vapor, burnt crema color, dirty cup edges
```

### C. Iced Coffee (Layered)

```
Using uploaded image as reference.

Professional food photography of iced coffee from reference,
showing beautiful milk/coffee layers and ice.

Side angle, back lighting for glass transparency,
condensation on glass acceptable, layers clearly visible,
fresh cold aesthetic.

4:5 or 9:16 aspect ratio.

--no steam (obviously), melted watery look, cloudy unclear layers
```

### D. Coffee + Pastry Pairing

```
Using uploaded images: Image 1 for coffee, Image 2 for pastry.

Lifestyle food photography of coffee and pastry pairing.
Coffee as supporting element (40%), pastry as hero (60%) OR equal focus.

Morning breakfast atmosphere, warm natural light,
complementary placement, shallow DOF,
inviting "come enjoy" mood.

4:5 vertical.

--no steam, smoke, human hands, cluttered table, cold lighting
```

### E. Pour Over Action (Aksiyon - Özel)

```
Using uploaded image as reference.

Professional food photography of pour over coffee brewing from reference,
water stream visible, kettle in frame.

Action moment frozen, coffee dripping into carafe,
dramatic but clean lighting, craft coffee aesthetic.

4:5 or 9:16 aspect ratio.

CRITICAL: Show water stream, NOT steam. Liquid only.

--no steam, smoke, vapor, blurry water stream, messy drips
```

### F. Coffee Flight/Trio

```
Using uploaded images as reference.

Professional food photography of coffee flight from reference,
3 different coffee drinks arranged in line or triangle.

45-degree angle, even lighting across all cups,
variety visible (espresso, latte, cappuccino),
clean coordinated presentation.

4:5 or 1:1 aspect ratio.

--no steam, smoke, inconsistent lighting, random arrangement
```

## Negatif Prompt Kütüphanesi

### Universal + STEAM YASAĞI

```
--no steam, smoke, vapor, mist, fog, hot steam rising,
dark background, black backdrop, grey background,
human hands, fingers, human elements,
harsh shadows, flash photography,
CGI look, artificial, plastic appearance,
busy background, cluttered composition,
watermark, logo overlay,
different product than reference
```

### Kahve Spesifik

```
--no steam, smoke, vapor (TEKRAR - ÖNEMLİ),
watery weak coffee, broken crema, flat foam,
dirty cup rim, lipstick marks, spills,
cold dead appearance, grey coffee color,
blurry latte art, melted ice (for iced), cloudy layers
```

### Latte Art Koruması

```
--no blurry foam pattern, broken latte art design,
foam bubbles disrupting pattern, asymmetric pour,
different pattern than reference
```
