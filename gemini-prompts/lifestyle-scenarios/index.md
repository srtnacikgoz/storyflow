### El Kuralları (Detaylı)

```
HAND REQUIREMENTS:
- Elegant feminine hand with well-manicured nails
- Nail colors: nude, soft pink, or French manicure
- Accessories: thin gold/silver ring, delicate bracelet
- Hand position: natural, relaxed grip
- Skin: smooth, well-moisturized appearance
```

### Universal Negative Prompt

```
--no masculine hands, chipped nail polish, bitten nails,
chunky jewelry, hairy arms, visible veins,
awkward hand position, stiff fingers,
dark background, harsh shadows, steam, smoke,
CGI look, artificial appearance
```

## Detaylı Promptlar

### 1. Zarif Tutma

**Konsept:** Bakımlı tırnaklı kadın eli kruvasan/pasta tutuyor, parmağında ince yüzük, soft focus arka plan

**Ürünler:** Kruvasan, Pasta dilimi, Macaron, Çikolata

```
Using uploaded image as reference for the product.

Lifestyle Instagram story photo of elegant feminine hand holding [ÜRÜN] from reference.

HAND STYLING:
- Well-manicured nails with nude/soft pink polish
- Thin gold ring on ring finger
- Smooth, moisturized skin
- Natural relaxed grip, fingers gently curved

COMPOSITION:
- Product held at slight angle toward camera
- Hand entering frame from bottom-right
- Product fills 40% of frame
- Soft blurred background (f/2.0)

LIGHTING:
- Soft natural side light from left
- Warm golden tones
- Gentle highlights on nail surface
- No harsh shadows on hand

ATMOSPHERE:
- Intimate, personal moment
- Morning freshness feel
- Warm, inviting mood

9:16 vertical for Instagram Stories. 8K photorealistic.

--no masculine hands, chipped nails, harsh shadows, dark background,
steam, smoke, awkward grip, stiff fingers, visible veins
```

### 2. Kahve Anı

**Konsept:** Manikürlü eller latte fincanını kavruyor, yanında macaron/çikolata, bilekte ince bilezik

**Ürünler:** Latte + Macaron, Cappuccino + Çikolata, Kahve + Kruvasan

```
Using uploaded images: Image 1 for pastry, Image 2 for coffee cup.

Lifestyle Instagram story of feminine hands cradling coffee cup,
with [ÜRÜN] from Image 1 visible on saucer or beside.

HAND STYLING:
- Both hands visible, cupping the coffee
- French manicure or nude nails
- Delicate gold bracelet on wrist
- Thin ring visible on one hand

COMPOSITION:
- Coffee cup as secondary focus (30%)
- Pastry visible but slightly soft (20%)
- Hands prominent (50%)
- Top-down slight angle (70 degrees)
- Cozy table surface visible

LIGHTING:
- Warm morning window light
- Soft steam-free presentation (NO STEAM)
- Golden hour warmth
- Gentle shadows

ATMOSPHERE:
- Cozy morning ritual
- Self-care moment
- Peaceful, unhurried

9:16 vertical for Instagram Stories. 8K photorealistic.

--no steam, smoke, masculine hands, cold lighting,
harsh shadows, chipped polish, busy background
```

### 3. Hediye Açılışı

**Konsept:** Zarif parmaklar çikolata kutusunu açıyor, tırnaklar nude renk, yüzük parıltısı

**Ürünler:** Çikolata kutusu, Macaron kutusu, Pasta kutusu

```
Using uploaded image as reference for the gift box.

Lifestyle Instagram story capturing the moment of opening a gift box,
elegant feminine hands lifting the lid to reveal [ÜRÜN] inside.

HAND STYLING:
- Graceful finger positioning on box lid
- Nude or soft pink nail polish
- Engagement-style ring catching light
- Delicate wrist, thin bracelet visible

COMPOSITION:
- Hands at top of frame, opening motion
- Box contents partially revealed
- Anticipation moment frozen
- Shallow depth of field on hands

LIGHTING:
- Soft diffused light
- Subtle sparkle on ring
- Even illumination on box contents
- No harsh reflections

CRITICAL TYPOGRAPHY:
- Brand name on box must remain sharp and legible
- Logo undistorted

ATMOSPHERE:
- Gift-receiving excitement
- Luxurious unboxing
- Special occasion feel

9:16 vertical for Instagram Stories. 8K photorealistic.

--no text deformation, blurry brand name, masculine hands,
harsh shadows, cluttered background, damaged box
```

**Ürünler:** Cheesecake, Layer Cake, Tart, Mousse

```

Lifestyle Instagram story of feminine hand with fork,
taking the first bite from [ÜRÜN] slice on white plate.

HAND STYLING:
- Elegant grip on silver/gold fork
- Nails visible, well-manicured
- One ring, simple and elegant
- Natural hand angle

COMPOSITION:
- Fork piercing cake surface
- Action moment - about to lift first bite
- Cake slice as hero (60%)
- Hand and fork (40%)
- White plate, minimal styling

LIGHTING:
- Soft overhead with side fill
- Highlights on fork tines
- Cake texture visible
- Warm, appetizing tones

ACTION:
- Fork mid-pierce or lifting
- Cake layers visible at cut point
- Texture revealed

ATMOSPHERE:
- Anticipation of first taste
- Indulgent moment
- Clean, elegant presentation

9:16 vertical for Instagram Stories. 8K photorealistic.

--no messy plate, crumbs everywhere, masculine hands,
harsh shadows, plastic fork, dark background
```

```

styled on white/grey marble surface with gold accents.

STYLING:
- White Carrara or grey marble surface
- Gold-trimmed small plate or stand

- Minimal negative space

COMPOSITION:

- Marble veining visible but not distracting
- Clean lines, geometric feel
- Generous white space

LIGHTING:
- Soft even studio-style light
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Gold elements catching light
- No harsh reflections on marble

ATMOSPHERE:
- High-end patisserie
- Luxury boutique feel
- Instagram-worthy minimal
- Clean and sophisticated

9:16 vertical for Instagram Stories. 8K photorealistic.

--no busy marble pattern, tarnished gold, cluttered props,
harsh shadows, fingerprints on marble, dust
```

```
STYLING:

- Clean table surface

LIGHTING:
```
